## What is the Dark Web and Why Onion Links Matter
The dark web is a hidden layer of the internet accessible only through specialized tools like the Tor browser. Unlike the surface web, it uses .onion domains for anonymity, shielding users and site operators from traditional surveillance. In 2026, with increasing cyber threats and regulatory scrutiny, reliable onion links are crucial for safe navigation.

Onion links point to these hidden services, often forums, marketplaces, or resources focused on privacy. However, the ecosystem is volatile—sites frequently change addresses due to takedowns or DDoS attacks. This guide focuses on verified, up-to-date links to help users avoid scams and dead ends.

### Key Risks and Precautions
Before diving in:
- **Use Tor Properly**: Download from the official site (torproject.org) and verify your setup.
- **VPN Layering**: Combine Tor with a no-logs VPN for extra protection.
- **Avoid Personal Data**: Never share real info or download untrusted files.
- **Legal Awareness**: Dark web access is legal in many places, but content varies—stay compliant.
